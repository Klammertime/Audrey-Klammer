About

I bring a range of skills to each project by drawing upon my years in marketing and creative roles.

Audrey Klammer

Web Developer & Marketer

My current obsession is with learning all I can about JavaScript, UX design and creating fun jQuery plugins. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Communication from UC Santa Barbara, a 1-year Multimedia Certificate from Front Range Community College and recently graduated from a 12-week Web Development Bootcamp, WebLab.

Over the past nine years, I've worked for a collection of forward-thinking companies ranging from an artificial intelligence startup recently acquired by IBM Watson to a newspaper edited by media pundit Dan Savage. During that time, I've been able to hone my skills in web design, digital marketing and communications.

I created this site using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Sublime, and Google Fonts. It takes advantage of responsive web design techniques including responsive typography.
